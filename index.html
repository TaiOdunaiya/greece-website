WHAT TO DO IN GREECE?

Visit Elafonissi Beach

On Crete's southwest coast, this remote beach is famed for its pink sand, though its intensity varies. Crystal-clear waters attract sunbathers and watersport enthusiasts. Hike to nearby cedar reserves for a change of scenery. Visit early for a spot before the crowds or in the evening for a beautiful sunset.

Visit Athens

History surrounds you here, from the Acropolis to the Parthenon. Wander through Plaka to savor traditional Greek dishes like moussaka or join a local food tour for hidden culinary gems. For a personalized experience, try our AI trip-planning product to craft your perfect itinerary.

Visit Village of Lindos

Perched on a hilltop by a stunning bay and beach, Lindos is known for its historical and natural beauty. Relax at St. Paul's Bay, visit the Acropolis, and explore local museums. Donkey rides offer a traditional way to navigate the steep hills. In summer, driving is tricky, so consider a ferry, catamaran, or bus from Rhodes Town.
